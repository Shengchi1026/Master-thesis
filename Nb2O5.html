Mechanistic Study of Lithium diffusion in H-Nb2O5

In previous reasearch, niobium oxide was regarded as a new Li-ion battery materials.

In my reasearch, i was calculated chemisty coefficient. Using four constants to research Lithium activity in H-Nb2O5 material.


1.Adsortion results

H-Nb2O5 have three major adsorption sites. One was pocket, others was vertiral and horizontal.

After compare each sites adsorion energy, we found that pocket had most strongest adsorption site.

2.Migration barrier

Second, I explored lithium migration barrier in static calculation.

Results show that lithum will pay largest energy from pocket to pocket.

3.Diffusivity

Third, lithium dynanic behavior was included. In this step, we found Li favor diffuse in horizontal (b-axis)
